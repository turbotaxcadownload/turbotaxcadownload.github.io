    TurboTax License Code

    Steps to Download and Install Turbotax


    1. Go to the official TurboTax website.
    2. Sign in to your TurboTax account.
    3. Enter your license code.
    4. Choose your operating system and click "Get Download."
    5. Wait for the download to complete.
    6. Double-click the downloaded file.
    7. Install the software.
    8. Enter your license code during activation.
    9. Click "Continue" to finish the installation.
    10. TurboTax is now ready to use.

    Activation Process of Turbotax


    1. Once installed, open TurboTax.
    2. Click "Activate" on the welcome screen.
    3. Enter your license code.
    4. Click "Activate" again.
    5. Follow prompts, provide information, verify identity.
